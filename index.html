For this interface, I wanted to allow viewers to explore the space around them using their own bodies through ml5.js BodySegmentation models. I applied live video processing to split the viewer's body into parts and represent those parts in various shapes, colors, sizes, and distances from the body.

Mind Mapping

I then mind mapped the ideas I explored earlier in the braindump to solidify an idea of what I wanted to express, and landed on the following:

Everything is perception. People perceive different parts of their body in different ways, trying to break people down into just shapes and colors that don't perfectly fit into their physical form
